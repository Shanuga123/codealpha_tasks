Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Indulge in a quick and flavorful Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables. Begin by thinly slicing 1 lb of boneless, skinless chicken breasts. In a wok or large skillet over high heat, cook the chicken until browned and thoroughly cooked. Set the cooked chicken aside and stir-fry 2 cups of broccoli florets, 1 thinly sliced red bell pepper, and a julienned carrot until they reach a crisp-tender consistency. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the vegetables, and then reintroduce the cooked chicken to the wok.

Delicios Classic Fruit Salad

Prepare a delightful Classic Fruit Salad by combining 2 cups of fresh strawberries, halved, with 1 cup each of blueberries and halved grapes, along with 2 peeled and sliced kiwi fruits, 1 peeled and segmented orange, a sliced banana, and 1 cup of pineapple chunks in a large bowl. Optionally, drizzle 1 tablespoon of honey over the fruits for added sweetness. Gently toss the fruits until well combined, ensuring an even distribution of honey. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld and the salad chill.
