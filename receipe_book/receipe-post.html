Delicios Homemade Spaghetti Bolognese

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Sauté the Vegetables: In a large pan or pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions, garlic, carrot, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.

02.

Brown the Meat: Add the ground beef to the pan. Break it up with a spoon and cook until it's browned.

03.

Add Tomato Paste and Wine: Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a couple of minutes. If using wine, add it to the pan and let it simmer until it reduces slightly.

04.

Add Crushed Tomatoes and Herbs: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the oregano, basil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

05.

Simmer and Add Liquid: Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Pour in the milk and broth, stirring to combine. Simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes.

06.

Cook the Spaghetti: While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain.

07.

Combine and Serve: Toss the cooked spaghetti with the Bolognese sauce until well coated. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley, if desired.

Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Prepare the Chicken:Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the sliced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Once cooked, set the chicken aside on a plate.

02.

Stir-Fry Vegetables:In the same wok, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Stir-fry the broccoli, red bell pepper, and julienned carrot until they reach a crisp-tender consistency. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

03.

Add Garlic and Ginger:Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the vegetables. Stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

04.

Combine Chicken and Sauce:Return the cooked chicken to the wok with the vegetables. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together until well coated and heated through.

05.

Finish and Serve:Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. If you like a bit of heat, you can add a pinch of red pepper flakes. Serve the Chicken Stir-Fry over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.

Delicios Classic Fruit Salad

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Prepare the Fruits:Wash and prepare all the fruits as needed. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.

02.

Combine Fruits:In a large mixing bowl, combine the pineapple chunks, strawberries, green grapes, cantaloupe, blueberries, and honeydew melon.

03.

Add Honey:Drizzle honey over the fruits. Adjust the amount of honey according to your sweetness preference.

04.

Gently Toss:Gently toss the fruits and honey together until the fruits are evenly coated.

05.

Prevent Browning of Banana:If you're adding banana slices, toss them in fresh lemon juice to prevent browning.

06.

Assemble:Add the banana slices to the fruit mixture just before serving to keep them fresh.

07.

Garnish and Serve:Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a burst of color and extra freshness (optional). Serve the classic fruit salad chilled.

Eggplant Parmesan Stacks

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Prep the Eggplant:Lay out the eggplant slices on a paper towel and sprinkle both sides with salt. Let them sit for about 30 minutes to draw out excess moisture. After 30 minutes, pat the eggplant slices dry with paper towels.

02.

Coat and Bread the Eggplant:Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs. Dip each eggplant slice first in the flour, shaking off excess, then in the egg, and finally in the breadcrumbs, pressing the breadcrumbs onto the eggplant to adhere.

03.

Cook the Eggplant:Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).Heat a skillet over medium-high heat with a few tablespoons of olive oil. Cook the breaded eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side. Place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess

04.

Assemble the Stacks:In a baking dish, spoon a small amount of marinara sauce to coat the bottom. Place a slice of eggplant, add a spoonful of marinara sauce, sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layering until you reach the desired height.

05.

Bake:Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

06.

Garnish and Serve:Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve the Eggplant Parmesan Stacks hot. They can be enjoyed on their own or over a bed of cooked pasta.

07.

Garnish and Serve:Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a burst of color and extra freshness (optional). Serve the classic fruit salad chilled.

Delicios Classic Fruit Salad

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Sauté Aromatics:In a large pot, heat coconut oil or olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion, garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté until the onion is translucent.

02.

Add Vegetables:Add diced carrot and bell pepper to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

03.

Spices and Curry Paste:Stir in the red curry paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, and turmeric powder. Cook for another minute to allow the spices to bloom.

04.

Simmer:Bring the soup to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the lentils are tender.

05.

Prevent Browning of Banana:If you're adding banana slices, toss them in fresh lemon juice to prevent browning.

06.

Assemble:Add the banana slices to the fruit mixture just before serving to keep them fresh.

07.

Garnish and Serve:Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a burst of color and extra freshness (optional). Serve the classic fruit salad chilled.

Mango Salsa Chicken Tacos

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Prepare Mango Salsa:In a bowl, combine diced mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, cilantro, jalapeño, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Refrigerate while preparing the chicken to let the flavors meld.

02.

Grill the Chicken:Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, ground cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to create a marinade. Brush the chicken breasts with the marinade on both sides. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side or until fully cooked and juices run clear. Once cooked, let the chicken rest for a few minutes before slicing it into strips.

03.

Assemble Tacos:Warm the tortillas according to package instructions. Place a layer of shredded lettuce on each tortilla. Top with grilled chicken strips. Spoon generous amounts of mango salsa over the chicken. Sprinkle crumbled feta or queso fresco on top. Garnish with additional cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

04.

Serve and Enjoy:Serve these Mango Salsa Chicken Tacos immediately, and encourage everyone to squeeze fresh lime juice over the top before devouring!

Teriyaki Salmon Bowls with Avocado and Quinoa

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Prepare the Teriyaki Marinade:In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, mirin, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger.

02.

Marinate the Salmon:Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag. Pour half of the teriyaki marinade over the salmon, reserving the rest for later. Marinate the salmon for at least 15-30 minutes.

03.

Cook the Quinoa:In a medium saucepan, combine quinoa, water (or vegetable broth), and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Fluff the quinoa with a fork.

04.

Grill or Pan-Sear the Salmon:Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the salmon for about 3-4 minutes per side or until cooked to your liking. Alternatively, you can pan-sear the salmon in a skillet with a bit of oil.

05.

Prepare the Bowls:In serving bowls, assemble the bowls with a base of cooked quinoa. Top with mixed greens, sliced avocado, cucumber, radishes, and green onions.

06.

Finish and Serve:Place the grilled teriyaki salmon on top of the bowls. Drizzle the remaining teriyaki sauce over the salmon. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with lime wedges on the side.

07.

Enjoy:Serve these Teriyaki Salmon Bowls immediately, and enjoy the combination of flavors and textures.
